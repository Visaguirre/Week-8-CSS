Dino Facts

  • The T. rex had a bite force of almost 12,800lbs!! Thats like 13 Grand Pianos slamming down at once.
  • The Carnotaurus had a top speed of 25 miles per hour.
  • The Mosasaurus could be up to 56 ft long. Thats almost 3 times as large as the biggest Great White Shark.
  • The Quetzalcoatlus (KWET-zal-koh-AT-lus)was named after a legendary feathered serpent god of Mexicoand was the size of a small airplane.
  • Some ancestors of the Triceratops were the same size as a large house cat weighing in at just 30 lbs.
  • Even though they weighed 4 tons and were over 30 ft long, they had a brain the size of a Walnut
  • The Brachiosaurus was discovered in 1900, by Elmer S. Riggs in Colorado, USA.
  • The Velociraptor was a ferocious pack hunter often hiding in the shadows of the hallway waiting for an unsuspecting shoeless father to fall prey to its trap.
